Good to know

Questions,
answered.

Everything clients most often ask us, from safety and downtime to booking and pricing. Can't find your answer? WhatsApp us any time.

Do I need a consultation before booking a treatment?
For injectables and doctor led treatments, yes, and it is the part of the process we take most seriously. Your doctor assesses your features, discusses your goals and designs a plan before anything is done. Facials and maintenance treatments can usually be booked directly.
Are your doctors qualified and certified?
Yes. All Soie treatments are performed by qualified medical professionals working to strict safety and hygiene protocols, using licensed, premium products and medical-grade devices.
Will my results look natural?
Natural results are the Soie signature. We favour conservative dosing, gradual building and balance over dramatic change. The goal is you, rested and refined, not a different face.
How do I book an appointment?
Use our online booking page to choose your branch, doctor, treatment, date and time. The request is sent via WhatsApp and confirmed personally by our team. You can also simply message either branch directly.
Where are you located and what are your hours?
We have two branches: UMC 216 in New Cairo and 41st Lebanon Street in Mohandseen. Both are open seven days a week: 10 AM to 11 PM Saturday through Thursday, and 12 PM to 10 PM on Friday.
Do treatments hurt?
Comfort is engineered into everything we do: numbing creams for injectables, cooled laser handpieces and genuinely gentle techniques. Most clients are surprised by how easy their treatment feels.
How much do treatments cost?
Pricing depends on the treatment, the products used and your personal plan, so we quote precisely after consultation. Message us on WhatsApp for current pricing on any treatment.
Is there any downtime after treatments?
Many of our treatments, including Hydrafacial, facials, laser and INDIBA body contouring, have no downtime at all. Injectables can show small marks or mild swelling for a few days. Every treatment page on this site lists honest recovery expectations.
Can I see examples of your results?
Yes. Our Before & After page shows real Soie clients, shared with their consent. Your doctor can show you further relevant examples during consultation.
Do you treat both women and men?
Absolutely. Our doctors design plans around each face and goal. Natural, discreet results are just as popular with our male clients.

Still curious? Ask us.

Message either branch on WhatsApp and a real member of our team will answer, usually within minutes.
