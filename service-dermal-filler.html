Dermal Filler

Restores volume and contour to lips, cheeks, jawline and under-eyes with balanced precision.

About the treatment

Dermal fillers are smooth gels of hyaluronic acid, a substance your skin already produces, used to restore lost volume, refine contours and hydrate from within. Lips, cheeks, jawline, chin, under-eyes and nasolabial folds can all be treated.

Our doctors design filler treatments around facial harmony: studying your proportions first, then placing small, structural amounts where they restore balance. The goal is always a face that looks like you: rested, lifted, never overfilled.

Benefits

  • Immediate, visible restoration of volume and contour
  • Natural feel and movement with premium HA fillers
  • Fully reversible, as hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved
  • Stimulates hydration and collagen over time
  • Tailored to your facial proportions, never a template

The treatment process

  1. Consultation and assessment. Your doctor listens first, then examines, and confirms that Dermal Filler is genuinely right for you.
  2. A personal plan. Together you agree the areas, the products and the outcome you can expect, with honest advice throughout.
  3. The treatment itself. Performed without hurry, using premium products and every comfort measure we have.
  4. Aftercare and follow up. You leave with clear guidance, and we see you again to make sure the result is exactly right.

Expected results

Volume and contour are visible immediately and refine as any swelling settles over two weeks, leaving a balanced, natural result that lasts six to eighteen months depending on the area and product.

Recovery & downtime

Mild swelling, tenderness or small bruises can appear for 2–5 days (lips swell the most in the first 48 hours). Most clients go straight back to daily life. Avoid intense exercise, alcohol and high heat for 24–48 hours, and massage the area only if your doctor asks you to. The final, settled result shows at about two weeks.

Who is it for?

Adults noticing volume loss, asymmetry, or contours they would like refined, from subtle lip hydration to jawline definition. A consultation determines the right product and amount; we treat conservatively and build gradually where needed.

Frequently asked questions

Will my lips or face look overdone?
Not at Soie. We plan in millilitres rather than syringes, placing small amounts precisely and reviewing before adding more. Natural balance is our signature.
Is filler safe?
In experienced medical hands, using premium CE-approved hyaluronic-acid products, filler is very safe. Our doctors are trained in facial anatomy and complication management, and HA filler is fully dissolvable.
What is the difference between Botox and filler?
Botox relaxes the muscles that crease the skin; filler restores volume and shape beneath it. They solve different problems and are often beautifully complementary.
