Treatment

Hydrafacial

Deep cleansing, gentle exfoliation and intense hydration in one signature glass-skin treatment.

About the treatment

Hydrafacial is the signature 'glass skin' treatment: a patented vortex technology that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts and floods the skin with hydrating serums in one seamless session. It is deep-working yet completely gentle.

Impurities are lifted from the pores while antioxidants, peptides and hyaluronic acid are infused, so you leave with skin that is visibly clearer, plumper and luminous, with zero downtime.

Benefits

  • Instant, camera-ready glow with zero downtime
  • Painless deep-pore cleansing and extraction
  • Intense hydration with hyaluronic acid and peptides
  • Softens fine lines and refines skin texture
  • Suitable for almost every skin type, even sensitive

The treatment process

  1. Consultation and assessment. Your doctor listens first, then examines, and confirms that Hydrafacial is genuinely right for you.
  2. A personal plan. Together you agree the areas, the products and the outcome you can expect, with honest advice throughout.
  3. The treatment itself. Performed without hurry, using premium products and every comfort measure we have.
  4. Aftercare and follow up. You leave with clear guidance, and we see you again to make sure the result is exactly right.

Expected results

Clearer, intensely hydrated “glass skin” the moment you leave, with the glow peaking over the following one to three days.

Recovery & downtime

None. Skin may look slightly flushed for an hour or two, then settles into its characteristic glow. Makeup can be applied the same day, though most clients don't want to cover the result.

Who is it for?

Almost everyone. Dull, congested, dehydrated, oily and sensitive skin all respond beautifully. It is the treatment we most often recommend before weddings, shoots and special occasions.

Frequently asked questions

Does Hydrafacial hurt?
Not at all; most clients find it genuinely relaxing. The extraction is done by gentle vortex suction, not squeezing.
When will I see results?
Immediately. Skin looks clearer and more hydrated the moment you leave, with the glow peaking over the next one to three days.
How often should I have one?
A monthly rhythm keeps skin consistently clear and hydrated; a single session before a big event works beautifully too.
