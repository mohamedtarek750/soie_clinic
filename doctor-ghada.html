About Dr. Ghada

Dr. Ghada leads the health and body-wellness side of SOIE Clinic, where medical aesthetics meets everyday wellbeing. Her philosophy is simple: the most beautiful results begin with a healthy body — balanced nutrition, a well-supported metabolism and treatments that work with your physiology, not against it.

Through her public platform she shares practical, medically grounded guidance on topics such as vitamin timing and absorption, diet and insulin resistance, abdominal bloating and sustainable weight management — alongside the in-clinic treatments she is known for, including non-surgical double-chin sculpting, cellulite treatment and lymphatic drainage massage.
