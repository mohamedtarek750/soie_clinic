About Dr. Ghada Metwally

“A blissful facial journey” — that is how Dr. Ghada Metwally describes her practice, and it is exactly what her patients experience. Based in New Cairo, she has built her work around medical facials that combine deep, clinical skincare with the calm, unhurried atmosphere SOIE is known for.

Her treatment menu spans the modern facial spectrum: HydraFacial deep cleansing and hydration, Dermapen microneedling for texture and collagen renewal, OxyGeneo oxygenating facials, medical-grade chemical peels and laser skin treatments. Her approach favours balance — refreshing and restoring the skin while keeping every feature natural.
