Body Contouring

Non-invasive sculpting and firming with the advanced INDIBA radiofrequency device.

About the treatment

SOIE's body contouring is built around INDIBA — an advanced radiofrequency technology that gently raises the temperature deep in the tissue, stimulating circulation, collagen production and the metabolism of stubborn fat, entirely without surgery.

Sessions feel like a warm, deep massage. Over a course, treated areas become firmer, smoother and more defined — making INDIBA a favourite for cellulite, post-weight-loss firming, double-chin refinement and pre-summer sculpting. Paired with lymphatic drainage and nutrition guidance, results compound.

Benefits

  • Non-invasive — no needles, surgery or downtime
  • Firms, smooths and helps reduce cellulite
  • Stimulates circulation and collagen renewal
  • Comfortable, warm sessions many find relaxing
  • Pairs with lymphatic drainage and nutrition plans

Recovery & downtime

None — this is a true no-downtime treatment. Skin may look pleasantly flushed for an hour. Hydrating well before and after each session supports the lymphatic effect.

Who is it for?

Ideal for localised stubborn areas, skin laxity, cellulite and post-weight-change firming — in clients close to their comfortable weight. It complements, rather than replaces, a healthy routine; our wellness team can support both sides.

Frequently asked questions

Does INDIBA hurt?
No — quite the opposite. The sensation is deep, comfortable warmth; many clients describe sessions as relaxing.
Is this a weight-loss treatment?
No. Contouring refines shape, firmness and skin quality in targeted areas. For weight management itself, our wellness side offers medical nutrition guidance — the two work beautifully together.
How many sessions will I need?
Most protocols run six to ten sessions, one to two weeks apart, then occasional maintenance. Your plan is tailored at consultation.
